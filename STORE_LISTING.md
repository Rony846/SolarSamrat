## Description
**Solar Samrat is where India's solar trade comes together.**

Whether you're a dealer, distributor, EPC/installer, brand or solar buyer, Solar
Samrat connects the whole industry in one place — share, source, sell and learn.

**🤝 Community**
Follow a live feed of installs, tips and wins. Join groups for residential, C&I,
off-grid, subsidy & policy, and troubleshooting. Ask the trade anything in Q&A
and get answers from people who've done it.

**📋 Leads & RFQs**
Post a product RFQ or a project lead and let the network come to you. Send quotes
on others' requirements and win new business — every response builds your name.

**⚡ AI Solar Quote**
Size any rooftop system in seconds. Enter the bill or units and get the system
size, full BOM, PM Surya Ghar subsidy estimate and payback — close customers on
the spot.

**👑 Climb the ranks**
Earn Crowns for being helpful and trusted. Rise from Sipahi to Samrat, top the
state and national leaderboards, and wear your Verified badge with pride.

Open to the whole solar industry. Free to join. Built in India, for India's solar
boom.
